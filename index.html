Welcome to YlePodcast.

YlePodcast is a school project created by students from Metropolia. Project goal is create a fully functioning podcast player, which use YleAPI to get playable podcasts.

Features

  • Login / Register
  • Create your own playlists
  • View your recently played and unfinished podcasts
  • Offline play your playlists
  • Search by title/duration/description or tags

Technical data

YlePodcast is programmed using Swift3 on the front-end (iPhone) and Node.JS on the backend. Podcasts are parsed from YleAPI using Alamofire framework. We are also adding lots of information from podcasts to CoreData to keep the app loading time small and speeding up the search functions. The podcasts are loaded asynchronously, to keep the app as fast as possible the first time you start the app.
